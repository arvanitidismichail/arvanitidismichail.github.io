Dr Michail Arvanitidis

PT, PhD, MSc, MMACP

Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Centre of Precision Rehabilitation for Spinal Pain (CPR Spine), School of Sport, Exercise and Rehabilitation Sciences, University of Birmingham, United Kingdom. My current research examines cervical adaptations in NASA astronauts following spaceflight, aiming to enhance astronaut health for future missions. My main interests involve studying motor adaptations in pain-affected or vulnerable populations.
