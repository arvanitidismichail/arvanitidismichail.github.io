Dr Michail Arvanitidis

PT, PhD, MSc, MMACP
Birmingham, United Kingdom 🇬🇧

My current research examines cervical adaptations in NASA astronauts following spaceflight, aiming to enhance astronaut health for future missions. Additionally, my main interests involve studying motor adaptations in individuals with persistent musculoskeletal pain.
